About me

I am a self-taught Software Developer from Brazil, focused on building and improving web based products using javascript (React & Redux, Node & Express). I am passionate about science and technology to improve the world, with a particular interest in automation, DLT/Blockchain, deep & machine learning (AI).

In my experience I learned how to establish structured processes to save money and improve performance from the ground up and really manage business in a sustainable manner. My last job was as a Product Manager & Owner, leading the design, development (front end) and design team. We went from 4 to 9 products, revenues from 10,000 to 100,000, serving the public and private sector, starting to expand abroad, winning startup awards and participating in investment rounds.

After that experience I decided to fully understand and learn the best web material to focus my career on building and coding Web Products for any kind of business. With the DevSecOps mindset I developed, I'm positive that I will help you and your team to improve the performance of your apps/products.

For the past 15 years, I've spent most of my time studying and working on ideas and visions of the future I want to build and live on.

My passion for science graduated me in Biological Sciences (2008) plus 2 years living abroad (2010). When I returned, I joined the largest environmental company in Brazil (2011) to learn about sustainable business and spent 6 years working on topics such as performance (KPI & BI systems), corporate management, logistics, financial and administrative planning. There, I learned how to establish structured processes to save money and improve performance from the ground up and really manage business in a sustainable manner.

Soon after (2017), I was looking for a career change more focused on my passion for technology, then I started learning about Web Development, Blockchain / DLT and IT Security. Lucky for me, I had the opportunity to work at a startup that did it all together. I became a product manager and owner, leading the design, development and design team. I was completely focused on working to improve and develop their business products while building the team for it. In one year, we went from 4 to 9 products, revenues from 10,000 to 100,000, serving the public and private sector, starting to expand abroad, winning startup awards and participating in investment rounds.

But it wasn't yet what I was looking for in my career. That's when I decided to get even more involved with technology, improving and learning new skills every day. I dedicated the last 2 years focused on fullstack web development (see Skills). Now I work as a freelancer for new business/startups. Recently I've been looking about Iot development with DLT technology called IOTA and in my spare time, I'm learning about Python for automation, deep & machine learning (AI) and OSCP.

With the DevSecOps mindset I developed, I'm positive that I will help you and your team to improve the performance of your apps/products.
